About-Me

How It All Started

Hello, I'm Shreya Kadam, a TY BSc IT student, and this is a little more about who I am beyond the basics. I've always admired websites and games, and wondered how they were actually built. That curiosity led me to coding — it felt hard at first, but I made up my mind to pursue it anyway. I explored which field would give me the right coding knowledge and chose IT after 12th, and looking back, it's been a good choice.

My First Step

Since this is my first project, I chose something small and simple — a way to build my foundation without feeling overwhelmed or giving up midway. It feels good to complete something I started. I'm currently learning full-stack web development through my college and a Udemy course, and I want to strengthen those skills through practical, hands-on projects. Starting small like this feels like the right first step toward eventually building something bigger and more real.

How I Solve Problems

Whenever I get stuck on an error or something I haven't learned yet, I like to research and understand it myself before looking anywhere else — why it's happening, what change might fix it. I do use resources like AI sometimes, but I try to solve things on my own first, because I don't want to lose my own thinking in the process. It usually takes time, but if I stop trying, I know I'll slowly lose interest and won't want to start another project. For me, coding isn't just about building things — it's equally about analyzing and solving problems.
